how to play

  • goal: score more points than the stranger.
  • duration: four one-minute rounds.
  • gameplay: enter as many words as possible that satisfy a clue.
    • sample clue: starts with A answers: apple, awesome, activate, etc.
    • blocking: whoever gets a word first gets points. If the stranger enters the word before you, you get no points, and vice versa.
    • obscure = more points: you get more points for less commonly used words.

rules

  • 4+ letter words
  • no proper nouns
  • no hyphenated words
  • no variations (e.g. plurals) of a played word
