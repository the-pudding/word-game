How to Play

  • Goal: score more points than the Word Opponent of the Day (WOD).
  • Duration: Four one-minute rounds.
  • Gameplay: Enter as many words as possible that satisfy a clue.
    • Sample clue: words that start with A . Answers: apple, awesome, activate, etc.
    • Blocking! Whoever gets a word first gets points. If the WOD entered the word before you, you get no points. If you enter a word before the WOD, she gets no points.
    • The more obscure the better! You get more points for less commonly used words.

Rules

  • 4+ letter words
  • No proper nouns
  • No hyphenated words
  • No variations (e.g. plurals) of a played word
