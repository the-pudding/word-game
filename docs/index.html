Title TK Word Game

One person a day gets the chance to play against the world in a game of words.

Today that person is Michelle from Oakland, CA. She really likes boba. Can you beat her?

Do you want to be the Opponent Of The Day? Sign up here to be in the running.

How to Play

  • Goal: score more points than The Opponent Of The Day (TOOTD).
  • Duration: Four one-minute rounds.
  • Gameplay: Enter as many words as possible that satisfy a clue.
    • Sample clue: words that start with A . Answers: apple, awesome, activate, etc.
    • Blocking! Whoever gets a word first gets points. If TOOTD entered the word before you, you get no points. If you enter a word before TOOTD, she gets no points.
    • The more obscure the better! You get more points for less commonly used words.

Rules

  • 4+ letter words
  • No proper nouns
  • No hyphenated words
  • No variations (e.g. plurals) of a played word
